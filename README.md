# Perfecting-IAM-until-I-get-Hired

Today marks the first day of my personal Challenge : Perfecting IAM Until I Get Hired. It’s a promise I’m making to myself. 

Every day, I’ll show up with intention, whether it’s through hands‑on labs, detailed study notes, or focused preparation for my Microsoft SC‑300 exam in September. 

I want to see how far consistent effort can take me when I fully commit.

I’m also stepping outside the comfort of solo learning. 

I want to understand IAM not just from documentation, but from the people who work with it every day. 

I’ll be reaching out to professionals in the field, not actually to ask for referrals, but to listen, learn, and understand what IAM looks like in the real world. 

I’ll be attending meetups, conferences, and community events to stay connected to what’s evolving in this domain and to surround myself with people who inspire me.

I’m documenting this journey publicly on GitHub and LinkedIn because I want to hold myself accountable and create a transparent record of my growth. LETS GO!
